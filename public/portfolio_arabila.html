Arabila

The project boasts a prime location in New Cairo, offering both elegance and convenience. It is just minutes away from Sokhna and Kattameya Road, Gamal Abdel Nasser Corridor, and the Ring Road, ensuring seamless connectivity. The development is also near key residential areas such as Al Narges and Gardenia and is adjacent to The Egyptian School, the German University (GUC), and New Cairo Academy. Additionally, it is situated beside Arabella Plaza Mall and in close proximity to essential service hubs, including Seven Star Mall and Silver Star Mall in the Fifth Settlement.

Designed with sophistication in mind, the project features Al-Nemr Royal facades, renowned for their distinctive and luxurious character in New Cairo villas. The grand entrances are adorned with imported marble and exquisite decorative elements, while all staircases are crafted from premium marble. The property is equipped with high-end imported elevators featuring unique door designs and aluminum architectural facades.

For enhanced security and comfort, the development includes sturdy building fences with crystal iron details, providing both safety and aesthetic appeal. A comprehensive security system is in place, incorporating surveillance cameras, a visual intercom, and designated air-conditioning holders with proper drainage. The landscape surrounding the project adds a touch of greenery, complemented by interlocking pathways leading to the building corridors, ensuring a refined and comfortable living experience.
